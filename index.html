Adventures in Fire Island

This past weekend, I explored the stunning Fire Island National Seashore. We took a ferry from Bay Shore, which was a short drive from New York City. The ferry ride was smooth, and we enjoyed the beautiful views of the water. We had a blast going to the beach and relaxing all day. The weather was perfect, and we even spotted some dolphins swimming nearby. We also visited the Fire Island Lighthouse, which offered breathtaking views of the surrounding area. The hike to the lighthouse was a bit challenging, but the scenery made it worth it. We also explored the charming town of Ocean Beach, where we found some great local shops and restaurants. The seafood was fresh and delicious, and we enjoyed a lovely dinner by the water. Overall, it was a fantastic trip, and I can't wait to go back to Fire Island.
